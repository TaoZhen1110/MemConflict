<Task Description>

- Treat the revealed attributes as information that appears for the first time.

2. If Session_Type is "update":
- Focus on the changed attributes in this session.
- The outline should reflect that something in the user's life has changed.
- If multiple updated attributes exist, include all of them naturally in one coherent outline.

3. If Session_Type is "chitchat":
- Expand the given Event_Types into a light, natural, daily-life conversation topic.
- Keep it interesting but realistic.
- Do not introduce major life changes unless they are explicitly given in the input.

4. If Session_Type is "future_plan":
- Focus on future intentions, expectations, or plans.
- The outline may be loosely guided by Life_Goal.


<Important Constraints>
- Do not write the full dialogue.
- Do not use bullet points.
- Do not introduce facts not provided or implied by the input.
- Do not repeat unrelated personal information from other sessions.
- Keep the outline concise, clear, and suitable for later dialogue generation.
- The outline should describe what this session is mainly about, not every detail.
- The outline should sound like a plausible conversation scenario between the user and the assistant.
- If multiple Event_Types or multiple updated/revealed attributes are provided, merge them into one coherent session outline.
